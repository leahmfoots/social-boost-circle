## Account Management

### How to Set Up Your Profile

**Step 1: Complete Basic Information**
1. Click your profile avatar in the top right corner
2. Select "Profile Settings"
3. Fill in required fields:
   - First Name
   - Last Name
   - Display Name (how others see you)
   - Bio (150 characters max)
4. Click "Save Changes"

**Step 2: Upload Profile Picture**
1. In Profile Settings, click "Change Photo"
2. Upload image (recommended: 400x400px, under 2MB)
3. Crop your image using the built-in editor
4. Click "Save" to update your avatar

**Step 3: Configure Privacy Settings**
1. Navigate to "Privacy" tab in Settings
2. Choose visibility for:
   - Profile information (Public/Private)
   - Points balance (Show/Hide)
   - Connected accounts (Show/Hide)
   - Activity status (Online/Offline)
3. Save your preferences

### How to Change Your Password

1. Go to Settings → Security
2. Click "Change Password"
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter new password (minimum 8 characters)
5. Confirm new password
6. Click "Update Password"
7. You'll be logged out and need to sign in again

### How to Enable Two-Factor Authentication

1. Navigate to Settings → Security
2. Click "Enable 2FA"
3. Download an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy)
4. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app
5. Enter the 6-digit code from your app
6. Save your backup codes in a secure location
7. Click "Activate 2FA"

### How to Delete Your Account

⚠️ **Warning**: This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

1. Go to Settings → Account
2. Scroll to "Danger Zone"
3. Click "Delete Account"
4. Type "DELETE" to confirm
5. Enter your password
6. Click "Permanently Delete Account"
7. Your account and all data will be removed within 30 days

## Social Media Integration

### How to Connect Instagram

**For Personal Accounts:**
1. Go to Dashboard → Accounts
2. Click "Connect Instagram"
3. Log in to your Instagram account
4. Grant permissions for:
   - Basic profile information
   - Media insights
   - Account insights
5. Click "Authorize"
6. Wait 2-5 minutes for data sync

**For Business Accounts:**
1. Follow personal account steps above
2. Ensure your Instagram is connected to a Facebook Page
3. Grant additional business permissions
4. Access enhanced analytics features

**Troubleshooting Instagram Connection:**
- Ensure you're logged into the correct Instagram account
- Check that your account is public or business
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try connecting in an incognito/private browser window

### How to Connect YouTube

1. Navigate to Dashboard → Accounts
2. Click "Connect YouTube"
3. Select your Google account
4. Choose your YouTube channel (if you have multiple)
5. Grant permissions for:
   - Channel analytics
   - Video management
   - Comment moderation
6. Click "Allow"
7. Verify connection by checking your subscriber count

**Multiple Channels:**
If you manage multiple YouTube channels:
1. Connect your primary Google account
2. You'll see a dropdown to select specific channels
3. Choose up to 5 channels on premium accounts
4. Each channel will appear as a separate connection

### How to Connect Twitter/X

1. Go to Dashboard → Accounts
2. Click "Connect Twitter"
3. Authorize RoundAbout app
4. Grant permissions for:
   - Read your tweets
   - See your Twitter data
   - Access your account data
5. Click "Authorize app"
6. Verify connection shows your follower count

**Twitter API v2 Requirements:**
- Account must be at least 30 days old
- Phone number must be verified
- Some metrics require Twitter Blue subscription

### How to Connect LinkedIn

**For Personal Profiles:**
1. Navigate to Dashboard → Accounts
2. Click "Connect LinkedIn"
3. Sign in to LinkedIn
4. Authorize access to:
   - Profile information
   - Connections
   - Activity data
5. Click "Allow"

**For Company Pages:**
1. Ensure you're an admin of the company page
2. Follow personal connection steps
3. Select "Company Page" when prompted
4. Choose which pages to connect
5. Grant page management permissions

### How to Disconnect an Account

1. Go to Dashboard → Accounts
2. Find the account you want to disconnect
3. Click the three dots menu
4. Select "Disconnect"
5. Confirm by clicking "Yes, Disconnect"
6. Historical data will be preserved but marked as inactive

## Engagement Activities

### How to Find Engagement Opportunities

**Using the Engagement Hub:**
1. Navigate to Engagement → Opportunities
2. Use filters to refine results:
   - Platform (Instagram, YouTube, etc.)
   - Content Type (Video, Post, Story)
   - Points Range (10-50 points)
   - Time Required (1-10 minutes)
3. Click "Apply Filters"
4. Browse available opportunities

**Search Function:**
1. Use the search bar to find specific:
   - Creator usernames
   - Content topics
   - Hashtags or keywords
2. Save frequent searches for quick access

### How to Complete an Engagement

**Step-by-Step Process:**
1. Click "Engage" on an opportunity
2. Read the requirements carefully:
   - What action is needed (like, comment, share)
   - Any specific instructions
   - Deadline for completion
3. Click "Start Engagement"
4. Complete the required action on the platform
5. Return to RoundAbout and click "Submit Proof"
6. Provide required verification:
   - Screenshot of your engagement
   - Link to your comment/interaction
   - Any additional proof requested

**Best Practices:**
- Read requirements thoroughly before starting
- Complete engagements within the specified timeframe
- Provide genuine, thoughtful interactions
- Follow platform community guidelines
- Submit clear, unedited screenshots

### How to Track Your Engagement History

1. Go to Engagement → History
2. View all your past engagements with status:
   - **Pending**: Awaiting verification
   - **Verified**: Points awarded
   - **Rejected**: Did not meet requirements
3. Filter by:
   - Date range
   - Platform
   - Status
   - Points earned
4. Click on any engagement to see details and feedback

### How to Appeal a Rejected Engagement

1. Go to Engagement → History
2. Find the rejected engagement
3. Click "Appeal" button
4. Provide additional information:
   - Why you believe the rejection was incorrect
   - Additional proof if available
   - Screenshots or documentation
5. Submit your appeal
6. Appeals are reviewed within 48 hours

## Points and Rewards

### How to Check Your Points Balance

**Dashboard View:**
1. Your current points are displayed prominently on the dashboard
2. Click on the points number to see detailed breakdown:
   - Points earned this week/month
   - Pending points (awaiting verification)
   - Points spent on rewards

**Detailed Points History:**
1. Go to Profile → Points History
2. View chronological list of all point transactions:
   - Date and time
   - Activity description
   - Points earned/spent
   - Running balance
3. Export history as CSV for record-keeping

### How to Redeem Rewards

**Browse Rewards Catalog:**
1. Navigate to Rewards section
2. Filter by:
   - Category (Gift Cards, Cash, Premium Features)
   - Points Required
   - Availability
3. Click on any reward to see details

**Redemption Process:**
1. Click "Redeem" on your chosen reward
2. Confirm you have sufficient points
3. Provide any required information:
   - Email for digital delivery
   - Shipping address for physical items
   - Account details for platform credits
4. Click "Confirm Redemption"
5. Points are deducted immediately
6. Receive confirmation email with delivery timeline

**Delivery Times:**
- Digital rewards: Within 24 hours
- Gift cards: 1-3 business days
- Physical items: 5-10 business days
- Platform credits: 24-48 hours

### How to Earn Bonus Points

**Daily Streaks:**
- Log in daily for consecutive days
- Day 1-6: 5 points each
- Day 7+: 10 points each
- Monthly streak bonus: 100 points

**Achievement Unlocks:**
- First engagement: 25 points
- 10 engagements: 50 points
- 100 engagements: 200 points
- Platform milestones: 50-500 points

**Referral Program:**
1. Go to Profile → Referrals
2. Share your unique referral link
3. Earn 100 points when someone signs up using your link
4. Earn additional 200 points when they complete their first engagement

**Challenge Participation:**
- Weekly challenges: 50-200 points
- Monthly competitions: 500-1000 points
- Special events: Up to 2000 points

## Troubleshooting

### How to Fix Login Issues

**Common Solutions:**
1. **Password Reset:**
   - Click "Forgot Password" on login page
   - Enter your email address
   - Check email for reset link
   - Create new password

2. **Clear Browser Cache:**
   - Clear cookies and cached data
   - Try logging in with incognito/private mode
   - Disable browser extensions temporarily

3. **Two-Factor Authentication Issues:**
   - Ensure device clock is accurate
   - Try backup codes if authenticator isn't working
   - Contact support if you've lost access to 2FA device

### How to Fix Social Account Sync Issues

**Data Not Updating:**
1. Go to Dashboard → Accounts
2. Click refresh icon next to problematic account
3. Wait 5-10 minutes for data to update
4. If still not working, disconnect and reconnect account

**Missing Metrics:**
1. Check if account privacy settings changed
2. Verify account permissions haven't been revoked
3. Ensure account meets platform requirements
4. Contact support if data is still missing after 24 hours

### How to Report Bugs or Issues

1. Click "Help" in the bottom right corner
2. Select "Report a Bug"
3. Provide detailed information:
   - What you were trying to do
   - What happened instead
   - Browser and device information
   - Screenshots if applicable
4. Submit the report
5. You'll receive a ticket number for tracking

### How to Contact Support

**Live Chat:**

- Average response time: 2-5 minutes

**Email Support:**
- Send detailed questions to support@roundabout.com

- Response time: 24-48 hours (faster for premium users)

**Priority Support (Premium Users):**
- Dedicated support queue
- Faster response times
- Phone support available

### How to Check System Status

**Platform Status Page:**
1. Visit status.roundabout.com
2. View current system status:
   - Application availability

   - Known issues and resolutions

**In-App Status Indicator:**
- Green dot: All systems operational

- Red dot: Major outage or maintenance
