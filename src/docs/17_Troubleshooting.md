## Account Access Issues

### I can't log in to my account

**Symptoms:**
- Invalid email/password errors
- Login button doesn't respond
- You get redirected back to the login page after submitting credentials

**Troubleshooting Steps:**

1. **Password Issues**
   - Check if Caps Lock is enabled
   - Ensure you're using the correct email address
   - Try the "Forgot Password" option to reset your password
   - Clear your browser cookies and cache

2. **Browser Issues**
   - Try a different browser
   - Disable browser extensions that might interfere
   - Enable cookies for roundabout.com

3. **Account Status Issues**
   - Check your email for account suspension notices
   - Verify your account hasn't been deactivated

4. **Advanced Troubleshooting**
   - Check if your IP is blocked (try from another network)
   - Ensure your account hasn't been compromised

### I didn't receive my verification email

**Troubleshooting Steps:**

1. Check your spam/junk folder and any filters you may have set up
2. Make sure you entered your email address correctly during registration
3. Add noreply@roundabout.com to your email contacts
4. Wait 15 minutes, as emails may be delayed
5. Use the "Resend Verification Email" option on the login page
6. Try registering with a different email provider if problems persist

### My account is locked

**Common Causes:**
- Multiple failed login attempts
- Suspicious activity detected
- Violation of terms of service
- Payment issues (for premium accounts)

**Troubleshooting Steps:**

1. Check your email for lock notifications and follow instructions provided
2. Wait 30 minutes, as temporary locks may automatically clear
3. Use the "Account Recovery" option if available
4. Contact support with your account details for manual review

## Social Account Connection Problems

### I can't connect my social media account

**Common Issues:**

1. **Authorization Failures**
   - The social platform is experiencing downtime
   - Your social account has restrictions
   - You didn't grant all required permissions

2. **Technical Issues**
   - Browser cookie/cache problems
   - API rate limits reached
   - Connectivity issues

**Troubleshooting Steps:**

1. Try connecting in a private/incognito browser window
2. Ensure you're using the most recent version of the browser
3. Try disconnecting and reconnecting the account
4. Check if your social media account is in good standing
5. Verify you have granted all requested permissions
6. Wait an hour and try again (API rate limits may reset)

### My connected account shows incorrect data

**Troubleshooting Steps:**

1. Verify the account was connected properly
2. Trigger a manual refresh:
   - Go to the Accounts page
   - Find the account with incorrect data
   - Click the "Refresh Data" button
3. Check if the account is public (private accounts may have limited data)
4. Verify the data is correct on the social platform itself
5. Allow 24 hours for data to fully sync, especially for new connections

### My account keeps disconnecting

**Troubleshooting Steps:**

1. Check for password changes on your social account
2. Verify you haven't revoked RoundAbout's access in the social platform's settings
3. Check for account restrictions or policy violations on the social platform
4. Ensure your account hasn't been deleted or temporarily suspended
5. Try removing and reconnecting the account completely

## Engagement Verification Issues

### My engagement wasn't verified

**Common Reasons:**

1. **Verification Issues**
   - The engagement doesn't meet the required criteria (too short, not relevant)
   - The proof provided is insufficient or unclear
   - The engagement can't be found or accessed

2. **Technical Issues**
   - Verification system delays
   - URL formatting problems
   - Platform API limitations

**Troubleshooting Steps:**

1. Check the verification requirements carefully
2. Ensure your proof is correct (screenshot or link)
3. Verify your engagement is public and accessible
4. Wait 24-48 hours as some verifications take longer
5. If rejected, review the reason and resubmit if appropriate

### Engagement was verified but I didn't receive points

**Troubleshooting Steps:**

1. Check your points history to verify if points were actually awarded
2. Look for notifications that might explain any issues
3. Verify the points value of the engagement (some opportunities have variable points)
4. Check if you've reached any daily engagement limits
5. Allow up to 1 hour for points to be credited after verification

### I can't submit proof of engagement

**Troubleshooting Steps:**

1. Check your internet connection
2. Try a different browser
3. Ensure your proof meets the format requirements:
   - URLs must include the protocol (https://)
   - Screenshots must be in supported formats (JPG, PNG, GIF)
   - File sizes must be under 5MB
4. Try copying the link directly from the address bar
5. If using screenshots, try using links instead or vice versa

## Points & Rewards Problems

### My points balance is incorrect

**Troubleshooting Steps:**

1. Review your points history for any discrepancies
2. Check for pending verification of engagements
3. Verify if points were deducted for reward redemptions
4. Check if promotional points have expired
5. Allow up to 24 hours for points to be properly reflected after activities

### I can't redeem a reward

**Common Issues:**

1. **Eligibility Issues**
   - Insufficient points
   - Account not in good standing
   - Reward unavailable in your region
   - You've reached a redemption limit

2. **Technical Issues**
   - Temporary system outage
   - Browser issues
   - Payment processing problems

**Troubleshooting Steps:**

1. Verify you have enough points for the reward
2. Check if the reward has any eligibility requirements
3. Try using a different browser
4. Clear your browser cache and cookies
5. Ensure you've completed your profile with any required information
6. Check if you've reached any redemption limits (daily, weekly, monthly)

### My reward was redeemed but not delivered

**Troubleshooting Steps:**

1. Check your email (including spam folder) for delivery information
2. Verify the delivery information you provided is correct
3. Check the estimated delivery timeframe (some rewards take 1-7 business days)
4. For digital rewards, verify they haven't been delivered to your RoundAbout inbox
5. Contact support if the delivery timeframe has passed

## Performance Issues

### The platform is slow or unresponsive

**Troubleshooting Steps:**

1. **Browser Issues**
   - Clear your browser cache and cookies
   - Close unnecessary tabs and applications
   - Update your browser to the latest version
   - Try a different browser

2. **Connection Issues**
   - Test your internet connection speed
   - Try a different network if possible
   - Disable VPNs or proxies temporarily

3. **Device Issues**
   - Restart your device
   - Close unused applications
   - Check for operating system updates

4. **Advanced Troubleshooting**
   - Disable browser extensions
   - Try incognito/private browsing mode
   - Check if the issue occurs across different devices

### Charts and data aren't loading correctly

**Troubleshooting Steps:**

1. Refresh the page
2. Clear browser cache
3. Ensure JavaScript is enabled in your browser
4. Check for browser extensions blocking scripts
5. Try a different browser
6. Check for system announcements about data issues

### Images or icons aren't displaying

**Troubleshooting Steps:**

1. Refresh the page
2. Clear browser cache and cookies
3. Check your ad blocker settings
4. Ensure your network isn't blocking image content
5. Try a different browser

## Mobile Experience Problems

### The platform doesn't work well on my mobile device

**Troubleshooting Steps:**

1. Ensure you're using a supported browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox)
2. Update your browser to the latest version
3. Enable JavaScript and cookies
4. Try rotating your device to landscape orientation for complex screens
5. Use the "Request Desktop Site" option for full functionality
6. Clear browser cache and cookies

### Features are missing on mobile

Some advanced features may have limited functionality on mobile devices. Try the following:

1. Switch to desktop view in your mobile browser
2. Use a tablet if available
3. Try a different mobile browser
4. Consider using the desktop version for complex tasks

## Notification Issues

### I'm not receiving notifications

**Troubleshooting Steps:**

1. **In-App Notifications**
   - Check your notification settings (Settings > Notifications)
   - Ensure you haven't muted specific notification types
   - Verify your account is in good standing
   - Check your internet connection

2. **Email Notifications**
   - Check your spam/junk folder
   - Add noreply@roundabout.com to your contacts
   - Verify your email address is correct in your profile
   - Check if you've unsubscribed from emails

3. **Browser Notifications**
   - Ensure you've allowed notifications in your browser
   - Check your browser notification settings
   - Verify your operating system notification settings

### I'm getting too many notifications

**Solution:**
1. Go to Settings > Notifications
2. Customize your notification preferences by:
   - Disabling specific notification types
   - Adjusting frequency settings
   - Setting quiet hours
   - Switching from real-time to digest mode

## Community & Groups Issues

### I can't post in the community

**Common Causes:**
- Your account is too new (waiting period for new users)
- Your account has posting restrictions due to previous violations
- You don't have sufficient points or reputation
- The group requires approval before posting

**Troubleshooting Steps:**

1. Check your account age (some features unlock after 3-7 days)
2. Verify you've completed your profile
3. Check for any restriction notifications
4. For groups, ensure your membership has been approved
5. Verify you haven't reached daily posting limits

### My community post was removed

**Common Reasons:**
- Violation of community guidelines
- Reported by multiple users
- Contains prohibited content (spam, self-promotion, etc.)
- Duplicates existing content

**Troubleshooting Steps:**

1. Check for notification explaining the removal
2. Review the community guidelines
3. Contact group moderators for specific group posts
4. Appeal the decision if you believe it was incorrect

### I can't join a specific group

**Troubleshooting Steps:**

1. Check if the group requires approval (allow 24-48 hours for moderator review)
2. Verify your account meets any group requirements (points, engagement level)
3. Ensure you haven't been previously removed from the group
4. Check if the group has reached membership capacity
5. Make sure your profile is complete and includes relevant information

## Still Having Issues?

If you've tried the troubleshooting steps and are still experiencing problems:

1. **Contact Support**
   - Email: support@roundabout.com
   - In-app chat support (available during business hours)
   - Support ticket: help.roundabout.com/submit-ticket

2. **Provide Detailed Information**
   - Your username or account email
   - Device and browser information
   - Screenshots of the issue
   - Steps to reproduce the problem
   - Error messages (if any)
   - When the issue started occurring

3. **Check System Status**
   - Visit status.roundabout.com for information about known issues and outages
